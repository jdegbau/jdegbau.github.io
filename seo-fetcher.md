  <h2 class="subtitle is-3">SEO Fetcher — Quick on-page SEO checks</h2>

  <p><strong>SEO Fetcher</strong> is a lightweight Chrome extension that surfaces important on-page SEO signals and metadata without leaving the tab. It's built for SEOs, content editors, and developers who want to inspect titles, meta descriptions, canonical tags, headers, and more at a glance.</p>

  <p><a class="button is-primary is-outlined" href="https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/seo-fetcher/jebfcaafldnmfgdbfdijbajhdmdapfnc" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Install SEO Fetcher on Chrome</a></p>

  <h3 class="title is-5 mt-4">What it does</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Fetches page metadata:</strong> title, meta description, meta robots, canonical URL.</li>
    <li><strong>Shows key headers:</strong> H1 and H2 content found on the page.</li>
    <li><strong>Flags common issues:</strong> missing titles/descriptions, duplicate canonicals, or disallowed robots directives.</li>
    <li><strong>Copy buttons:</strong> quickly copy meta or header values for notes or reporting.</li>
  </ul>

  <h3 class="title is-5 mt-4">Why I made it</h3>
  <p>Sometimes you need a fast answer: does this page have a good title, is the canonical correct, or is it blocked from indexing? SEO Fetcher gives concise, copyable output so you can work faster during audits and content reviews.</p>

  <h3 class="title is-5 mt-4">Privacy & permissions</h3>
  <p>The extension only reads the page you're visiting to extract metadata and header text. It does not collect or transmit page content back to any server — all analysis happens locally in your browser.</p>
